Who I am

From a young age I have thoroughly enjoyed creating things with my hands. From drawings of animals to LEGO cars and bridges, taking an idea residing in my mind and raising it from my mind to reality brings me great joy. Progressing through academia I found time to indulge my hobbies through elementary art exhibits, high school FIRST robotics, and collegiate student engineering competitions. Blending the skills I learned over the past decade I am now able to provide clients the ability to turn their ideas to reality.

Skills

Website Design, 2D design and 3D Modeling, Photography, Brand Design and Social Media
